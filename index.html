About Me

INTRODUCTIONS

I am a PhD Candidate in the ADEPT Lab at UC Berkeley, advised by Krste Asanovic and Jonathan Bachrach. I work primarily on tools to improve productivity in hardware design, including FIRRTL, a compiler framework for RTL design, and FireSim, an open-source FPGA simulation infrastructure that supports advanced resource optimzations and flexible co-simulation with software models.
